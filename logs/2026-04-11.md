### 💡 Learning Points
**Newly Acquired:**
- **Data Flow (One-Way Data Flow):** In React, the principle is that data (Props) flows unidirectionally from parent components (e.g., ProductTable) to child components (e.g., SearchBar), from top to bottom.

**Deepened Understanding:**
- **Role of Props:** Props are "read-only" and act like instructions passed from the parent. Understanding that `SearchBar` doesn't determine its own input values, but rather focuses on displaying the `filterText` Prop passed from its parent as the input `value` (rendering the UI).
