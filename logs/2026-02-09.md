### ğŸ’¡ Learning Points
**New Learnings:**
- **The True Nature of Closures:**
A mechanism that "remembers" the surrounding state (lexical environment) at the time a function is defined when the function is executed. It is the basis for creating variables that you do not want to directly access (private variables).

**Deepening Understanding:**
- **Why Closures Are Needed in Factory Functions:**
Unlike `Object Constructors` (which use the new keyword), Factory Functions use Closures to encapsulate (hide) internal variables and prevent unintended modifications. This is an investment that increases the robustness of the code.
